Ages 8-12: Fun & Fundamentals

For our youngest golfers, it's all about making golf an exciting and positive experience. We focus on building fundamental skills through engaging, low-pressure sessions that foster a lifelong love for the game. Parents will see their child develop character, discipline, and a healthy approach to competition.

  • Focus on enjoyment & basic mechanics.
  • Develop foundational golf skills.
  • Build character and sportsmanship.

Ages 13-16: Skill Refinement & Competitive Edge

This is where we refine existing skills and introduce more advanced strategies. For juniors aiming to lower scores or make their school team, our coaching emphasizes mental resilience, course management, and efficient practice routines. We prepare them for the next level of competitive play.
